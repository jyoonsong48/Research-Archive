## `DNA methylation-based age prediction from saliva: High age predictability by combination of 7 CpG markers`
Hong, S. R., Jung, S.-E., Lee, E. H., Shin, K.-J., Yang, W. I., & Lee, H. Y. (2017). DNA methylation-based age prediction from saliva: High age predictability by combination of 7 CpG markers. Forensic Science International: Genetics, 29, 118–125. https: //doi.org/10.1016/j.fsigen.2017.04.006
### `Keywords:`
Age, DNA methylation, Saliva, SNaPshot, HumanMethylation450 BeadChip
### `Summary`
7 CpG sites showed high correlation with age, even only with 4 ng of bisulfite-converted DNA. This will allow integration into routine forensic lab workflow after further validation tests with various samples. These CpG markers can further be used in other analysis platform: pyrosequencing, Next Generation Sequencing.
